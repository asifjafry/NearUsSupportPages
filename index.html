Externally Published Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

At NearUs Neighbors, we are deeply committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all users. The protection of children is a top priority, and we have zero tolerance for any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

Our CSAE Standards Include:

  • Zero Tolerance Policy: NearUs Neighbors strictly prohibits any content, conduct, or behavior that depicts, promotes, or facilitates the sexual exploitation or abuse of children.
  • Reporting & Safety Tools: Users can report suspicious or harmful content or behavior directly through the app. Our team reviews all reports promptly and takes appropriate action.
  • Moderation and Monitoring: We use automated detection tools along with human moderators to identify and remove CSAE-related content or accounts.
  • Age Restrictions & Protection: NearUs Neighbors is not intended for users under 13. We enforce age limits and monitor usage to ensure compliance.
  • Collaboration with Law Enforcement: We cooperate fully with law enforcement and child protection agencies such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
  • Technology to Prevent CSAE: Industry-standard hash-matching and scanning tools are used to prevent the upload of known exploitative material.
  • Community Awareness: We provide resources to help users identify and report CSAE and educate the community on online safety.
  • Strict Policy Enforcement: Accounts that violate these standards are permanently banned, and all CSAE content is removed and reported immediately.

Contact & Reporting: If you come across any content or behavior on NearUs Neighbors that raises concern, please report it through the app or email us at safety@nearusneighbors.com.
